INFORMATION THAT IS RELEVANT

* My concept was just to create the best card that ever existed, and not because of the status, but because I think Broly is the best character in the anime.

* The idea of ​​being Super Saiyan Broly and not Legendary Super Saiyan Broly is that this way, we can use more different types of teams, without worrying about link skills.

* The concept of creating Paragus is that it is practically not used by anyone, in this concept he would become a great support and ally for the "Big Bad Saiyans" Category and for Broly, as they both have very weak link skills.

* Broly Before Transformation have stats elevated because the category and link skills are very limited.

* This category is quite limited, so the stat of Ki +3 and HP, ATK & DEF +170%.

* The "Big Bad Saiyans" Category are all saiyans who have bad odjectives, all saiyans who are extreme.

* The Special Sticker of Super Saiyan Broly it could be something related to the spheres he has on his waist and head.

* After Transformation gains the "Big Bad Bosses" Link Skill.

* Link Skill Berserker works on HP is 20% or less (NERF) because Broly after trasnformation now have Big Bad Bosses Link Skill.
